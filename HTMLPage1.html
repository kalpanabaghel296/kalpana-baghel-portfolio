Extracurricular Activities:

Kappa League

Outside of school, I participated in the Alpharetta-Smyrna Alumni Chapter Guideright program (Kappa League). Kappa League is a mentoring program for young men where we learn valuable life lessons from members of the Kappa Alpha Psi fraternity. We learn about wide range of topics from public speaking & essay writing, to how to build meaningful relationships. I'm not currently participating in Kappa League as I'm in another program called Beautillion, which is also under the foundation that manages Kappa League.

Diamond Beautillion

During the 2022-2023 school year, I've been participating in the Krimson Community Foundation's Diamond Beautillion Program. I'd say it's like a more mature version of Kappa League, with a heavy focus on college preparation and becoming young men that are equipped to handle societies challenges. We had a formal ceremony on April 2nd, where we delivered speeches and were presented with scholarship money that we had to raise individually. It's was a great experience and I learned a lot.
